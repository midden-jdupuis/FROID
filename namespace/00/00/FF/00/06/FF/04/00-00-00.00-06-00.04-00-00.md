## Definition

A two-digit hexadecimal component of a [NODE_BLK]. A [NODE_BLK] is composed of an ordered sequence of [NODE_SEG]; a [NODE_ID] is composed of an ordered sequence of [NODE_BLK]. [NODE_SEG] is the atomic structural primitive of [FROID_NS] addressing.

## Relations
