```
node_id: 00-00-01.00-03-01.01-00-00
node_nam: Folio Manifest
node_tag: FLIO_REF
```

## Definition

[FLIO_REF] is the canonical manifest for one [FOLIO].

[FLIO_REF] carries the assigned [NODE_ID] of the [FOLIO] and provides its descriptive metadata. The filename of [FLIO_REF] is the [NODE_ID] of the [FOLIO] concatenated with the `.frd` extension. The [FLIO_REF] file resides in the leaf directory of the [FOLIO]'s [NODE_ID].

[FLIO_REF] contains required fields and optional fields.

Required fields: `node_id`, `node_nam`, `created`, `flo_bind`, `flo_auth`, `record_hash`.
Optional fields: `flio_pld`, `payload_hash`, `supersession_hash`, `status`, `flo_spup`, `flo_spdn`.

The hash fields (`record_hash`, `payload_hash`, `supersession_hash`) carry [FLO_HASH] values and are governed by [FLIO_HSH]. `record_hash` carries [FL_HSCOR], `payload_hash` carries [FL_HSPLD], `supersession_hash` carries [FL_HSSPR].

## Rules

- One [FLIO_REF] per assigned [FOLIO]; one [FOLIO] per valid, assigned [NODE_ID].
- [FLIO_REF] filename MUST exactly match the [NODE_ID] of the [FOLIO] with the `.frd` extension.
- Existence of a [FLIO_REF] at a [NODE_ID]'s leaf directory establishes the [FOLIO]; absence of [FLIO_REF] means no [FOLIO] is assigned, regardless of directory presence.
- The `flio_pld` field MAY grow over time as additional [FLIO_ART] are attached; the existing entries and their referenced files MUST NOT change.

## Constraints

- Required fields MUST all be present.
- `node_id` MUST equal the [NODE_ID] encoded in the filename.
- `flo_bind` MUST be one of [FL_STRCT] or [FL_OVRLY].
- `flo_auth` MUST be present and MUST be one of [FL_NORM] or [FL_DESC].

## Relations

- [FLIO_REF] is the manifest document for one [FOLIO].
- [FLIO_REF] identifies the [FOLIO] via its `node_id` field.
- [FLIO_REF] enumerates [FLIO_ART] via [FLIO_PLD].
- [FLIO_REF] records [FLIO_ATH], [FLIO_BND], [FLIO_HSH], and [FLIO_REL] of the [FOLIO].
