## Definition

[FLIO_OWN] is the field-in-manifest that records the [NODE_ID] of the [FOLIO] representing the owner of this [FOLIO].

The manifest field name is `flio_own`. Its value is a [NODE_ID] that resolves to an owner-shaped [FOLIO] elsewhere in [FROID_NS]. Owner folios live outside the [FOLIO] structural family (typically under their own BB branch for actors and principals).

[FLIO_OWN] has no [FLO_OWN] value class. Validation is reference-resolution rather than value-enumeration: the field's value is "any [NODE_ID] resolving to an owner-shaped [FOLIO]," not an entry in a closed list.

## Rules

- `flio_own`, when present, MUST be a [NODE_ID] resolving to an existing [FOLIO] in [FROID_NS].
- The referenced [FOLIO] is the owner of this [FOLIO] at commit time and is fixed thereafter.

## Constraints

- `flio_own` carries exactly one [NODE_ID].
- The referenced [FOLIO] MUST exist when the [FLIO_REF] is committed.

## Relations

- [FLIO_OWN] is recorded by [FLIO_REF].
- [FLIO_OWN] references one [FOLIO] (the owner).
- [FLIO_OWN] does NOT enumerate over a closed value class; it resolves by reference.
