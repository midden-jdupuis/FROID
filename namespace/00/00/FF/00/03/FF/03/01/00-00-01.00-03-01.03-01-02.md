## Definition

[FL_DESC] is the [FLO_AUTH] value indicating that a [FOLIO] carries non-normative descriptive content. Content within the [FOLIO] is informative; it does not constrain other [FOLIO].

## Relations

- [FL_DESC] is a member of [FLO_AUTH].
- [FL_DESC] is a permitted value of [FLIO_ATH].
