## Definition

[FL_NORM] is the [FLO_AUTH] value indicating that a [FOLIO] carries prescriptive normative content. Rules and constraints inside the [FOLIO] are binding within their scope.

## Relations

- [FL_NORM] is a member of [FLO_AUTH].
- [FL_NORM] is a permitted value of [FLIO_ATH].
