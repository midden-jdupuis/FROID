## Definition

[FLIO_ATH] is the authority designation of a [FOLIO]. It indicates whether the content carried by the [FOLIO] is normative or descriptive.

[FLIO_ATH] is recorded in the optional `flo_auth` field of [FLIO_REF]. Its value is drawn from the [FLO_AUTH] vocabulary class.

## Rules

- A [FOLIO]'s [FLIO_ATH], when assigned, is fixed at commit and immutable.
- A [FOLIO] without an explicit [FLIO_ATH] has no authority claim.

## Constraints

- The `flo_auth` field of [FLIO_REF] MUST be one of [FL_NORM] or [FL_DESC] when present.

## Relations

- [FLIO_ATH] values are enumerated by [FLO_AUTH].
- [FLIO_ATH] is recorded by [FLIO_REF].
- [FLIO_ATH] applies to one [FOLIO].
