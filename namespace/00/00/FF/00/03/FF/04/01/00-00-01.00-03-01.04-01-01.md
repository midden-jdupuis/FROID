## Definition

[FL_OVRLY] is the [FLO_BIND] value indicating that a [FOLIO] has no parent or child relation in [FROID_NS]. Its non-zero [SEG_GRAN] selector segments determine the subtree to which the [FOLIO] applies.

A [FL_OVRLY] [FOLIO] is a scoped rule or metadata artifact rather than a hierarchical concept node.

## Relations

- [FL_OVRLY] is a member of [FLO_BIND].
- [FL_OVRLY] is a permitted value of [FLIO_BND].
