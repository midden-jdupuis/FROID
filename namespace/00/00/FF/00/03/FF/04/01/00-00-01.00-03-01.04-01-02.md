## Definition

[FL_STRCT] is the [FLO_BIND] value indicating that a [FOLIO] participates in the primary [FROID_NS] structural tree.

A [FL_STRCT] [FOLIO] requires a full ancestor chain: every [FL_STRCT] identity node on the path from this [FOLIO]'s [NODE_ID] to the root MUST also be assigned.

## Relations

- [FL_STRCT] is a member of [FLO_BIND].
- [FL_STRCT] is a permitted value of [FLIO_BND].
- [FL_STRCT] [FOLIO] are organized hierarchically by [SEG_GRAN] segments.
