## Definition

[FLIO_BND] is the binding designation of a [FOLIO]. It determines whether the [FOLIO] participates in the primary [FROID_NS] structural tree or applies as a scoped rule.

[FLIO_BND] is recorded in the required `flo_bind` field of [FLIO_REF]. Its value is drawn from the [FLO_BIND] vocabulary class.

## Rules

- Every [FOLIO] has exactly one [FLIO_BND].
- A [FOLIO]'s [FLIO_BND] is fixed at commit and immutable.

## Constraints

- The `flo_bind` field of [FLIO_REF] MUST be one of [FL_STRCT] or [FL_OVRLY].

## Relations

- [FLIO_BND] values are enumerated by [FLO_BIND].
- [FLIO_BND] is recorded by [FLIO_REF].
- [FLIO_BND] applies to one [FOLIO].
