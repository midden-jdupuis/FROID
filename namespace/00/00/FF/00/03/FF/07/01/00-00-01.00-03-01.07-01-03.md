## Definition

[FL_HSSPR] is the [FLO_HASH] value indicating a hash appended at supersession time, chaining from [FL_HSCOR] and [FL_HSPLD] (if present) plus supersession metadata (the replaced [NODE_ID] and the supersession timestamp).

[FL_HSSPR] is carried by the `supersession_hash` field of [FLIO_REF].

Once [FL_HSSPR] is appended to a [FLIO_REF], no further write or supersession events are permitted on the [FOLIO]; the [FOLIO] is closed.

## Relations

- [FL_HSSPR] is a member of [FLO_HASH].
- [FL_HSSPR] is the value carried by `supersession_hash` in [FLIO_REF].
- [FL_HSSPR] terminates the lifecycle of a [FOLIO].
