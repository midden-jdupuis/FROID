## Definition

[FL_HSPLD] is the [FLO_HASH] value indicating a hash computed over [FLIO_PLD] entries in order at time of commit.

[FL_HSPLD] is carried by the `payload_hash` field of [FLIO_REF].

[FL_HSPLD] is required when [FLIO_PLD] is non-empty and MUST be omitted when [FLIO_PLD] is absent or empty.

## Relations

- [FL_HSPLD] is a member of [FLO_HASH].
- [FL_HSPLD] is the value carried by `payload_hash` in [FLIO_REF].
