## Definition

[FL_HSCOR] is the [FLO_HASH] value indicating a hash computed over the committed [FLIO_REF] manifest fields, excluding the [FLIO_HSH] hash fields themselves from the digest input.

[FL_HSCOR] is carried by the `record_hash` field of [FLIO_REF].

[FL_HSCOR] is required on every [FLIO_REF] regardless of whether [FLIO_PLD] is present.

## Relations

- [FL_HSCOR] is a member of [FLO_HASH].
- [FL_HSCOR] is the value carried by `record_hash` in [FLIO_REF].
