## Definition

[FLO_HASH] is the controlled vocabulary class enumerating the kinds of hashes [FLIO_HSH] may carry.

Members: [FL_HSCOR] (core), [FL_HSPLD] (payload), [FL_HSSPR] (supersession).

## Constraints

- The set of valid [FLIO_HSH] hash kinds is closed at this revision: [FL_HSCOR], [FL_HSPLD], [FL_HSSPR].

## Relations

- [FLO_HASH] enumerates [FLIO_HSH].
- [FLO_HASH] members are [FL_HSCOR], [FL_HSPLD], and [FL_HSSPR].
