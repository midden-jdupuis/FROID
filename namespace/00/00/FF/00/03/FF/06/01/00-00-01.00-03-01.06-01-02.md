## Definition

[FL_REFER] is the [FLO_RLTN] value indicating that one [FOLIO] references another non-superseding [FOLIO]. A reference is a non-replacing logical link.

## Relations

- [FL_REFER] is a member of [FLO_RLTN].
- [FL_REFER] relates two [FOLIO] without implying supersession or replacement.
