## Definition

[FLIO_REL] is the set of relations a [FOLIO] holds with other [FOLIO] in [FROID_NS].

[FLIO_REL] values are recorded in [FLIO_REF] via two structures. Supersession is governed by [FLIO_SUP] using the `flo_spup` and `flo_spdn` field pair (carrying [FL_SUPER] semantics). Reference relations carry [FL_REFER] values. Relation values are drawn from the [FLO_RLTN] vocabulary class.

## Rules

- A [FOLIO]'s [FLIO_REL], when assigned, is fixed at commit and immutable.
- A [FOLIO] without explicit [FLIO_REL] entries declares no relations.

## Constraints

- Relation entries MUST identify the related [FOLIO] by its [NODE_ID].

## Relations

- [FLIO_REL] values are enumerated by [FLO_RLTN].
- [FLIO_REL] is recorded by [FLIO_REF].
- [FLIO_REL] applies to one [FOLIO] but always references at least one other [FOLIO].
