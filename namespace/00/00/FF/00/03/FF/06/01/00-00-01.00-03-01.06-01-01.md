## Definition

[FL_SUPER] is the [FLO_RLTN] value indicating that one [FOLIO] supersedes another within the same lineage.

A superseding [FOLIO] declares the [NODE_ID] of each [FOLIO] it replaces in the `flo_spup` field of its [FLIO_REF]. The superseded [FOLIO]'s `flo_spdn` field is updated post-commit via the manifest-append discipline; the superseded [FOLIO]'s artifact content remains immutable.

## Relations

- [FL_SUPER] is a member of [FLO_RLTN].
- [FL_SUPER] relates two [FOLIO] in the same lineage: the superseding [FOLIO] and the superseded [FOLIO].
