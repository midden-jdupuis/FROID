## Definition

[FLO_RLTN] is the controlled vocabulary class enumerating the kinds of inter-folio relations [FLIO_REL] may declare.

Members: [FL_SUPER] (supersession), [FL_REFER] (reference).

## Constraints

- The set of valid [FLIO_REL] kinds is closed at this revision: [FL_SUPER], [FL_REFER].

## Relations

- [FLO_RLTN] enumerates [FLIO_REL].
- [FLO_RLTN] members are [FL_SUPER] and [FL_REFER].
