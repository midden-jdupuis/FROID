## Definition
[TIM_TIME] is a descripton of [PROJECT]
[TIM_TIME] encodes [UNIXEPOC] in [NODE_ID]

## Rules
[TIM_TIME] is constructed of 2 [NODE_BLK]
[UNIXEPOC] is converted from decimal to hexadecimal as <dec2hex> (ex 1)
<dec2hex> is cleaved into 2-digit <hexseg> (ex 2)
each <hexseg> is assigned to [NOD_SEG] big-endian and assigned to [NODE_ID] (ex 3)
[NOD_BIN] of first [NODE_BLK] is <bin1>
[NOD_BIN] of second [NODE_BLK] is <bin2>
<bin1> must be either `01` or `02`
<bin1> value of `01` is positive [UNIXEPOC]
<bin1> value of `02` is negative [UNIXEPOC]
<bin2> may take a value from `00` to `64`
<bin2> represents hundredths of a second in hexadecimal
