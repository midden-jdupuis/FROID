## Definition

- every [FOLIO] is described by [FLIO_ART] which have [FLIO_ATH] and [FLIO_BND]

## Rules

- [FOLIO] may have zero or one [FLO_INDX]
- [FOLIO] requires zero or one [FLO_MFST]
- if [FOLIO] has [FLO_MFST] it requires 1 and only one [FLO_TYPE] of [TYP_MOTO]
- [FOLIO] may have zero or more additional [FLIO_ART]
