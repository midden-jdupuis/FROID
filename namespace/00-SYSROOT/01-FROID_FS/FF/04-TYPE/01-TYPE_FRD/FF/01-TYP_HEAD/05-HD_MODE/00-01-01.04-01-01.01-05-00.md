```yaml
hd_nodid: 00-01-00.04-01-00.01-05-00
hd_nodtg: HD_MODE
hd_auth: FLO_NORM
hd_bind: FLO_STRC
hd_mode: MODE_ONE
```

## Definition
- [HD_MODE] is a [YAML] field containing the [MODE] of the [FOLIO]

## Rules
- [HD_MODE] must take one of the values: MODE_ONE, MODE_TWO, MODE_TRE, null

## Restrictions

## Reciprocals
- [HD_MODE] is the fifth field in [TYP_HEAD]

## Examples
- hd_mode: MODE_ONE
