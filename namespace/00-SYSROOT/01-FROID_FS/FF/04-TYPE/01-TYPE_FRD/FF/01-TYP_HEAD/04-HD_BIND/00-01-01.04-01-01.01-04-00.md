## Definition
- [HD_BIND] is a [YAML] field containing the [FLIO_BND] of the [FOLIO]

## Rules
- [HD_BIND] must take a value of either [FLO_STRC] or [FLO_OVER]

## Restrictions

## Reciprocals
- [HD_BIND] is the fourth field in [TYP_HEAD]

## Examples
- hd_bind: FLO_STRC
