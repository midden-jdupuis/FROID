## Definition
- [TYP_HEAD] is a [YAML] that describes [FOLIO]
- [TYP_HEAD] is defined by 00-01-01.04-01-02.01-00-00.yaml

## Rules
- [TYP_HEAD] contains the following fields in the following order:
  1. [HD_NODID]
  2. [HD_NODTG]
  3. [HD_AUTH]
  4. [HD_BIND]
  5. [HD_MODE]

## Restrictions
- [TYP_HEAD] does not describe [FOLIO] fully

## Reciprocals
- [TYP_HEAD] begins every [FLIO_ART]

## Examples
- hd_nodid: 00-01-00.04-01-00.01-00-00
- hd_nodtg: TYP_HEAD
- hd_auth: FLO_NORM
- hd_bind: FLO_STRC
- hd_mode: MODE_ONE
